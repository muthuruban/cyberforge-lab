# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and constructive open-source community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Share feedback constructively.
- Assume positive intent.
- Focus on helping others learn and improve.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Encouraging unsafe or unauthorised cyber activity

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participation for conduct violations.
